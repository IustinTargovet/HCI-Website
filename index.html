Intro

Our client, noticed that many of the UCL students would like to join the program but never find the time to go to sessions that would help them with the proccess and even more so, apply to the Study abroad scheme.

That's where we came into play and proposed a mobile app that eases the proccess up.

Client Requirements

The first order of business was talking to our client to list up the requirements for the application we were supposed to build. After our first sit down with the client we managed to line out these requirements:

  • Mobile app
  • Prospective students can input subjects and grades and return applicable institutions
  • Information about visa requirements
  • UCL API login; returns universities suitable for you/steps you need to take
  • Information about costs of different unis
  • Apply via app
  • Attach required documentation through app
  • See what course people are interested in List favourite institutions

User Requirements

We decided to also talk to our clients, UCL students to see what exactly they would expect from this our Application. These are the main concerns that every student seemed to care about:

  • Information about the suggested grades you will need to go to high/medium/low demand universities
  • Contact information for the external tutor for your course, and the study abroad team
  • List of FAQs about the study abroad program
  • A detailed list of all the next steps you need to take in your department and the deadlinese
